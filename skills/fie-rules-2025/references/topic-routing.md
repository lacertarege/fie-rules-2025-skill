# Topic Routing

Open this file after `official-documents.md` when the user asks a rule question and the right source is not obvious.

## Technical Rules

Use for:

- foil, epee, or sabre actions
- right of way or priority
- valid target and off-target consequences
- referee procedure and commands
- piste boundaries and leaving the piste
- bout timing, periods, priority minute, and start or halt procedure
- behavior during the bout

## Material Rules

Use for:

- weapon conformity
- masks, bibs, gloves, jackets, plastrons, breeches, socks, and shoes
- bodywires, reels, connectors, conductive equipment
- safety and inspection requirements
- spare equipment obligations

## Organisation Rules

Use for:

- pools and direct elimination
- team competition structure
- seeding, ranking, and event organization
- calls, reporting, and competition administration
- officials, delegations, and event procedures outside the bout itself

## Penalties and Cards

Use `t.170` first for:

- yellow, red, black, or P-cards
- offence group lookup
- quick penalty lookup by behavior

Then check the cited technical or other article if:

- the wording of the offense matters
- the case is unusual or disputed
- the user asks for exact interpretation

## Response Guardrails

- Say "under the FIE 2025 rules" when answering directly.
- If the question mixes training advice with formal rules, separate the rules answer from coaching advice.
- If a question asks whether something is "legal," specify whether legality depends on weapon, phase of competition, or equipment inspection.
